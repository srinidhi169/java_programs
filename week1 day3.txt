#write a algorithm to find factorial of positive integer number
1.start
2.read the number n
3.initialize factorial=1,i=1,count=1
4.repeatedly execute the steps i,ii,iii until(count<=n)
        (i) factorial=factorial*i
        (ii) i=i+1
        (iii) count=count+1
5.print factorial
6.stop
